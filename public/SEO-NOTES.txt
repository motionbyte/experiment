Google indexing — quick checklist
==================================

1) Domain in sitemap / robots
   Canonical site: https://thelostsymbols.in — keep sitemap/robots in sync with
   Google Search Console (same protocol: https; use www only if that is your property).

2) "Page with redirect" in Search Console
   Usually: http→https, www→non-www, or trailing-slash redirects.
   Fix: use ONE canonical URL everywhere (sitemap, internal links, GSC property).
   Avoid listing redirecting URLs in the sitemap — only list the final 200 OK URL.

3) Submit sitemap
   In Search Console: Sitemaps → https://YOURDOMAIN/sitemap.xml

4) 100% indexing
   Google never guarantees every URL will be indexed. A valid sitemap + clean redirects
   + good content helps. Album pages exist at /album/<id> for crawlers.

5) After adding albums
   Add a new <url> block in public/sitemap.xml for each new album id, or regenerate.
